About Me

Hi! My name is Aisyah Maisarah and you can call me ecah, and I was born on June 21, 2004. I am 20 years old and I have three older brothers, which makes me the only daughter and the youngest sibling in the family. Growing up as the youngest has been a unique experience, filled with love and guidance from my older brothers, who have always been protective and supportive of me.

I enjoy spending my time engaging in activities that bring me comfort and happiness.
